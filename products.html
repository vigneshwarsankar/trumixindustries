TRU Joints™ is well recognised brand in US, a leader in making innovative "Armoured Joints" now available in India. TRU Joints™ is India's first "Rust Free" Multi Layered "Epoxy Coated" Joints in Multi Color. Certified by NABL against salt proof spray test ensures robust Quality & TRU Value for Money.

TRU Dowel Plates™

TRU Dowel's - TRU Diamond Dowel sleeves are uniquely crafted and protected with Trademark, making them impossible for local dealers to replicate. They're made from 100% virgin polymers, which is essential, while many sleeves in India are made from reprocessed plastic that tends to be brittle and can fail as the concrete sets.

TRU PolyWRAP™ LDPE sheets

TRU PolyWRAP™ is a major provider of LDPE plastic sheets, which are utilized in a wide range of sectors. Our sheets are renowned for their remarkable strength and durability and are manufactured from premium materials. To fulfill the demands of every project, we provide a wide variety of sizes.

NEXACRETE™ Floor Hardener

NEXACRETE™ fulfils the demands of every FM2 floors that requires to withstand repetitive impact loads & rigorous abrasion from MHE's. Nexacrete™ offers India's first AR Class 0.5 and AR 1 non-metallic floor dry shake hardeners, which are formulated with specially selected high wear-resistant aggregates and a polymer-modified mix to ensure optimal adhesion to the underlying concrete. Additionally, Nexacrete™ incorporates microfibers that mitigate shrinkage effects and facilitate the application of multiple layers effectively.

Nexacrete Colour Hardeners

Transform Ordinary Floors into Extraordinary Spaces.No more dull, grey concrete! Nexacrete Colour Hardeners bring life and luxury to your floors with a stunning range of vibrant, customizable colours. Designed to enhance both beauty and durability, our hardeners create seamless, polished concrete finishes that stand the test of time.

SHRINKARE™ 3D (SRA)

A unique Shrinkage-Reducing Admixture specially formulated for Jointless / Seamless floors, Open floor Casting, large pour panels, and mass concrete sections. Helps to mitigate drying shrinkage cracks development from aggressive concrete hydration.

DensiCON™

A high-performance Nano Lithium Silicate densifier engineered for dust-free, polished concrete floors with long-lasting aesthetic appeal.Its ultra-fine Nano particle technology penetrates deeper into the concrete matrix, delivering superior hardness, enhanced durability, and a brilliant finish. The result? A maintenance-free, wear-resistant surface that stands the test of time.

Zigma HD 500™

High-Density Isolation & Expansion Pads made from zero-bitumen content resins, engineered to deliver unmatched compression recovery and exceptional flexibility. Ideal for isolating walls, trenches, machine pits, and columns, Zigma HD 500™️ effectively protects concrete floors from restraint and re-entrant cracking, ensuring long-term structural integrity and performance.

CUREXX™

CUREXX™️ is a ready-to-use, acrylic resin based curing compound designed to retain moisture in freshly placed concrete. Upon application, it forms a continuous, non-penetrating membrane that dries into a semi-gloss film, reducing water loss and enhancing cement hydration. The product exhibits excellent non-yellowing properties and complies with international standards such as ASTM C1315 Type 1 Class B and ASTM C309.
