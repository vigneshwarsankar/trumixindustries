About Us

TRUMIX INDUSTRIES is a global leader in the field of industrial flooring solutions, was founded by a team of professionals with expertise in Industrial Flooring Engineering, dedicated to delivering tailored contemporary design solutions for industrial flooring, ensuring high-quality products accompanied by comprehensive quality control and testing certificates.

TRU Joints™ is well recognised brand in US, a leader in making innovative "Armoured Joints" now available in India. TRU Joints™ is India's first "Rust Free" Multi Layered "Epoxy Coated" Joints in Multi Color. TRU Joints™ is well regarded by the American Concrete Society & complies with European standards such as TR34, EN codes etc.

*Caution - Buyers please be informed that we are the only sole authorized manufacturer of TRU Joints™ in India. Our product line includes Heavy Duty, Medium Duty, and Normal Duty joints, each available in various price ranges, which will be provided upon request. We also offer scratch-free packaging for every joint up on request, and we encourage customers to place orders at least a week in advance to ensure we fully understand the specifications and design modifications needed. Please do not hesitate to contact us for enhanced floor protection solutions.
